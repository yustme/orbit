How to use

  • Everything starts in the ☰ menu (top right). Select what you want to explore first — nothing happens without picking something from the menu.
    • Planets — Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune
    • Spacecraft — Voyager 1 & 2, Pioneer 10 & 11, Cassini, New Horizons, Juno, MESSENGER, Rosetta, Galileo, Ulysses, Dawn, BepiColombo, Mars Odyssey, Mars Express, Parker Solar
    • Galaxies — Andromeda, Triangulum, LMC, SMC, Bode's, Centaurus A, Sombrero, Whirlpool, M87, Sculptor, Keboola
  • Selected spacecraft: the camera follows it and shows its trajectory through time
  • Selected planet: zooms in and reveals its moons
  • Selected galaxy: flies out to it and shows info about it
  • Scroll to zoom in and out — also works while following a spacecraft or moon
  • Drag to rotate the view freely
  • Use the timeline at the bottom to travel through time from 1990 to 2050
  • Adjust speed to slow down or fast-forward the simulation
  • Click Photos to browse real flyby images taken by each spacecraft
  • ↺ resets everything back to the default state
